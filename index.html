About Morbik

Morbik Electric Vehicle Assembly Factory PLC is at the forefront of electric vehicle technology in Africa. Established in 2020, we are committed to producing eco-friendly and high-performance electric vehicles that meet global standards.

Our mission is to revolutionize transportation with innovative electric solutions for both personal and commercial use, helping reduce the carbon footprint and drive the future of sustainable mobility.

Services

Electric Vehicle Assembly

At Morbik, we specialize in the precise and efficient assembly of cutting-edge electric vehicles. Our advanced technology and skilled workforce ensure that every vehicle meets the highest standards of quality and performance. Whether it's a compact city car or a robust electric truck, we handle each assembly with meticulous attention to detail.

Battery Technology

Our battery technology is at the forefront of the industry, designed for optimal energy density and longevity. We offer state-of-the-art battery packs that enhance the range and reliability of electric vehicles. Our innovation in battery management systems ensures safety and efficiency, making your driving experience both reliable and eco-friendly.

Charging Solutions

Morbik provides comprehensive charging solutions tailored to meet the needs of modern electric vehicles. From home charging stations to public charging infrastructure, our solutions are designed to be user-friendly and efficient. We are committed to making the transition to electric driving seamless and convenient for everyone.
